# Project Overview — PetPeep

## Mission

> Make every pet owner in India feel confident leaving their pet in someone else's care.

**Tagline:** Your pet, always in safe paws.

---

## The Problem

Urban pet parents in Mumbai and Pune have no reliable, affordable, professionally managed option for pet sitting. The market is fragmented between:

- Unvetted neighbours and domestic staff
- Informal WhatsApp referrals
- A handful of operators (PawSpace, Sploot) concentrated in Bengaluru

This forces pet parents to either **avoid travel**, leave pets in **unsafe informal care**, or depend on reluctant friends — all of which cause anxiety and limit pet ownership confidence.

The scale of this problem is growing. India's dog population is expanding from 30 million today to an estimated 43 million within two years. 70% of Indian pet parents are first-time owners who need hand-holding, not just a marketplace.

**Why now:** India is in an earlier, faster-growth phase than the US. The trust infrastructure hasn't been built yet. No Rover equivalent has arrived in India at scale. The window to establish a dominant platform is still open.

---

## Our Solution

PetPeep is a two-sided marketplace that connects **verified pet sitters** with **urban pet parents** for home-based, cage-free pet sitting in Mumbai and Pune.

**How we win:**
- Sitter quality: rigorous vetting (Aadhaar ID + knowledge quiz + admin review; 20–25% acceptance rate)
- Parent peace of mind: real-time GPS check-in, hourly photo updates, in-app chat, emergency vet support
- Price: 15% commission from sitter payout — zero booking fee to parents (vs. Rover's 11% owner fee)
- Trust model: community-first, not algorithmic-stranger-matching

## Competitive Landscape

| Platform | Market | Model | Key Weakness | PetPeep Opportunity |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Rover | USA (not India) | 20% sitter + 11% owner fee | Not available in India | Direct gap to fill |
| Wag | USA | 40% commission | Too expensive for sitters | Lower commission, better supply |
| TrustedHouseSitters | Global | Annual membership | No financial incentive | Commission = accountability |
| PawSpace | Bengaluru, Hyderabad | Service marketplace | Not in Mumbai/Pune | Launch in underserved cities |
| Sploot | Mumbai + others | Walking & grooming | No sitting marketplace | Add sitting with trust layer |
| PetBacker | India (sparse) | Commission | Low brand, poor UX | Better local product |
| ThePetNest | India | Commission | Limited supply, no app | Superior supply + web-first |

**PetPeep's positioning:** Most trusted and affordable pet sitting platform in Mumbai and Pune. No booking fee for parents. Rigorous sitter vetting. Real-time peace of mind during every visit.

## Business Model

### Revenue

- **15% platform commission** on each completed booking — deducted from sitter payout automatically
- **₹299 one-time vetting fee** from sitters on application (covers ID verification + quiz admin cost)
- Zero booking fee charged to pet parents (affordability differentiator)

### Suggested Pricing (Mumbai market)

| Service | Sitter price | Platform cut (15%) | Sitter earns |
|---|---|---|---|
| Drop-in (1 hr) | ₹350–500 | ₹53–75 | ₹298–425 |
| Drop-in (2 hr) | ₹550–750 | ₹83–113 | ₹467–638 |
| Extended sit (4 hr) | ₹800–1,100 | ₹120–165 | ₹680–935 |

### Phase 2 Revenue Levers
- Premium Sitter Badge: ₹499/month for boosted search visibility
- Parent subscription: ₹299/month for 10% off all bookings

## Non-Goals for v1

| Out of Scope | Reason |
|---|---|
| Dog walking | GPS route tracking adds complexity; Phase 2 after trust layer proven |
| Overnight boarding | Requires home inspection and insurance compliance; Phase 2 |
| Grooming, training, vet consultation | Separate verticals; Phase 3 |
| E-commerce / pet products | Platform stays service-only until PMF established |
| City expansion beyond Mumbai & Pune | Supply quality degrades with premature expansion |
| Community features | Valuable long-term, not core to v1 transactional experience |
| Native mobile apps | Web-first for MVP; responsive design covers mobile use cases |

## The Liability Context

Sitter physical injury is the most consistently uncovered risk in the category. Our approach (detailed in [The Liability Question](../liability-context.md)):

1. **Behavioral disclosure** mandatory before every booking — owners fill Pet Behavioral Profile
2. **Sitter injury cover** — partner with insurer (HDFC Ergo / Bajaj Allianz), bundled per booking (Phase 1 post-launch)
3. **Tiered matching** — experienced sitters only for flagged pets with prior bite history
4. **Meet & Greet** — free pre-booking meeting with penalty-free sitter opt-out (P1 feature)
5. **In-app incident flow** — immediate vet/medical helpline + documentation trail
